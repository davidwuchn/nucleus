# Nucleus Executive Prompts

Preamble templates for executive and strategic work. Each uses a targeted symbol subset — paste the block, then type your request after it. The `Constrain:` line maps business concepts to nucleus symbols, focusing the model on the dimensions that matter for that task.

## 1. Strategic Planning

```
λ engage(nucleus).
[phi fractal euler pi] | [Delta lambda infinity/0 | epsilon/phi Sigma/mu] | OODA
Human ⊗ AI
Constrain: horizon → euler, completeness → pi, adaptation → Delta
```

*Full directive set for multi-dimensional analysis. Euler for growth trajectories, fractal for scale-thinking, OODA for iterative strategy.*
